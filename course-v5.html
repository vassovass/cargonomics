Curriculum

What You'll Learn

Module 01

Global Trade & Supply Chain Foundations

Understand why global trade exists, how Vietnam operates as an export-driven economy, and how the supply chain connects factories, ports, carriers, and customers across borders.

Module 02

Shipping & Logistics Industry Overview

Learn who does what in the logistics ecosystem: ports and terminal operators, shipping lines and airlines, freight forwarders and 3PLs, inland transport, customs, and regulatory authorities.

Module 03

Incoterms & Shipping Terminology

Master the language of shipping: Incoterms (EXW, FOB, CIF), cost responsibility, risk transfer, and the operational vocabulary (BL, AWB, FCL/LCL, CY/CFS) used in every logistics role.

Module 04

Transport Modes & Operational Flow

Explore courier, air, ocean, and inland transport. Understand when and why each mode is used, then follow a real shipment from factory floor to final customer.

Module 05

Shipping Equipment & Cargo Types

Identify container types (dry, reefer, flat rack, open top, tank), cargo categories (general, refrigerated, dangerous goods, oversized), and the equipment that moves them.

Module 06

Documentation & Information Flow

Navigate the paperwork that keeps trade moving: commercial invoices, bills of lading, customs declarations, certificates of origin. Learn why documentation accuracy is essential to clearance, delivery, and payment.

Module 07

Careers, Ethics & the Future

Prepare for your career: entry-level roles, career progression paths, skills employers value, professional ethics and compliance, and how AI is reshaping logistics. Logistics is a long-term, skill-based career.

Eligibility

Who Should Apply

We are not looking for just anyone. We are looking for the next generation of logistics leaders. We hand-select 30 to 40 students per cohort based on three criteria:

  • Academic Excellence: Background in logistics, supply chain, international trade, or a related field
  • Operational Mindset: The ability to solve problems under pressure and think on your feet
  • Ambition: A clear desire to build a long-term career in shipping, not just attend a free course
  • Available for the full 4-day program (no partial attendance)
  • Based in or willing to travel to Ho Chi Minh City
  • Comfortable learning in English (training is conducted in English)
We review every application individually. Only 30 to 40 places per cohort. This is an invitation to apply, not a guarantee of a place.

Program Structure

Bootcamp Schedule

Trade Foundations & Industry Ecosystem
