The Opportunity

Vietnam: A Rising Logistics Powerhouse

Ho Chi Minh City sits at the centre of one of the world's fastest-growing logistics corridors. Container throughput at Vietnamese ports has grown dramatically. New deep-water ports are being built. International shipping lines are increasing capacity. The demand for skilled logistics professionals is growing faster than the talent pipeline can deliver. Cargonomics exists at this intersection.
