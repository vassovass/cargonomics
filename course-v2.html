Flagship Program

What You Didn't Learn at School

A 5-day intensive shipping and logistics bootcamp designed for ambitious graduates ready to launch their careers. Industry-sponsored, highly selective, and built around the real skills that employers need.

Start Date
June 1, 2026
Duration
5 Days
Investment
Industry-Sponsored
Location
Ho Chi Minh City

This is not open enrollment. The WYDLS bootcamp is a selection-based program where each applicant is individually reviewed. Selected students gain direct access to industry leaders, hands-on port operations experience, and a pathway to placement with hiring companies across Vietnam's shipping and logistics sector.

Corporate partners fund the training because they trust Cargonomics to deliver graduates who are ready to contribute from day one. For students, that means no tuition, no fees, and a genuine career launchpad.

Curriculum

What You'll Learn

Module 01

Shipping & Logistics Landscape

Understand the global trade ecosystem: major shipping routes, international carriers, port networks, and Vietnam's growing role as a logistics hub in Southeast Asia.

Module 02

Operations & Supply Chain

Learn end-to-end logistics execution: container handling, freight forwarding, warehouse management, and how goods move from origin to destination.

Module 03

Sales & Commercial

Master the commercial side of shipping: pricing strategies, rate negotiation, customer acquisition, and how to build lasting business relationships in a competitive market.

Module 04

Documentation & Compliance

Navigate the paperwork that keeps trade moving: bills of lading, customs declarations, export/import documentation, and regulatory compliance across jurisdictions.

Module 05

Legal Frameworks

Study the rules that govern international trade: maritime law, trade agreements, liability and insurance, and how disputes are resolved in the shipping industry.

Module 06

Ethics & Career Paths

Prepare for your career: professional ethics, industry culture, interview preparation, and placement readiness. Includes a visit to a working dock and port facility.

Eligibility

Who Should Apply

The WYDLS bootcamp is designed for graduates and final-year students who want to enter the shipping and logistics industry. No prior experience is required, but we look for drive, commitment, and a genuine interest in building a career in this sector.

  • Recent graduate or final-year student in logistics, supply chain, business, international trade, or a related field
  • Available for the full 5-day program (no partial attendance)
  • Based in or willing to travel to Ho Chi Minh City
  • Comfortable learning in English (training is conducted in English)
  • Motivated by a career in shipping and logistics, not just a free course
  • Able to work evenings if currently employed (daytime attendance required)
We review every application individually. Spots are limited to ensure quality mentorship and meaningful interactions with industry professionals. This is an invitation to apply, not a guarantee of a place.

Program Structure

Bootcamp Schedule

Day 1
Orientation & The Shipping Landscape

Welcome and introductions. Overview of global trade routes, major carriers, and Vietnam's position in the international supply chain.

Day 2
Operations & Supply Chain

Container handling, freight forwarding, warehouse systems, and end-to-end logistics execution. Practical case studies from Vietnamese ports.

Day 3
Sales, Commercial & Documentation

Pricing strategies, customer acquisition, and rate negotiation. Bills of lading, customs declarations, and regulatory compliance.

Day 4
Legal Frameworks & Career Readiness

International maritime law, trade agreements, liability and insurance. Interview preparation and professional development planning.

Day 5
Dock & Port Facility Visit

A full day at a working port facility. See operations first-hand, meet professionals on the ground, and understand what a career in logistics looks like in practice.
