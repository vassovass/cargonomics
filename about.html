About Cargonomics

Coaching. Consulting. Connecting.

Cargonomics is where the shipping and logistics world comes to grow. We are a specialized partner for the industry, with a dedicated platform for Coaching, Consulting, and Connecting.

Driven by a team of dedicated professionals with over 50 years of combined experience, extensive international partnerships, and a strong local network, we help individuals and organizations navigate the complexities of global trade. Our solutions include expert-led training and courses, strategic consulting, recruitment and placement, business matching, and exclusive industry events.

Logistics is a global engine. Cargonomics provides the human element that keeps it running.

Our mission: To build a progressive and empowering knowledge-based company, empowering the Vietnamese shipping and logistics community through expert coaching, strategic consulting, and elite networking, setting a new global standard for local excellence.

Visit us: 268 To Hien Thanh Street, Hoa Hung Ward, District 10, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

What We Do

Three Pillars of Impact

01 / Coaching

Train. Assess. Place.

Our flagship bootcamps take fresh graduates and career changers through intensive, hands-on training delivered by industry veterans. Participants are assessed on technical knowledge, communication, and professional readiness. Top graduates are placed directly with hiring companies from our network of over 1,000 corporate connections. Coaching is the entry point to the Cargonomics ecosystem: the vetting mechanism that companies trust.

02 / Connecting

Events. Networks. Opportunities.

Cargonomics brings the shipping and logistics industry together through conferences, networking events, and business matching. Our inaugural conference at The Reverie Saigon drew over 300 attendees. These events create the relationships that power placements, partnerships, and industry growth. Connecting is the ecosystem that surrounds our training programmes.

03 / Consulting

Strategy. Insight. Growth.

For shipping and logistics businesses looking to grow, we offer strategic advisory services: market studies, feasibility analysis, financial evaluation, and operational consulting. Our consulting work is grounded in decades of hands-on industry leadership, not theory. Consulting is the revenue expansion arm that completes the Cargonomics model.
