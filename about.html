About Cargonomics

Cargonomics is a shipping and logistics training, consulting, and conferencing company based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. We exist to bridge the gap between academic logistics education and what the industry actually needs: professionals who can operate from day one.

Vietnam sits at the centre of one of the world's fastest-growing logistics corridors, yet universities continue to produce graduates who lack practical exposure to how shipping operations work. Cargonomics was created to solve that problem. Through intensive bootcamp training, industry mentoring, and direct placement with hiring companies, we take fresh graduates and career changers from zero industry knowledge to placement-ready in five days.

Cargonomics operates under the leadership of Abraham Y. Elias, who also serves as Executive Director of the Anne Hill Education Group. This connection to a broader education ecosystem reinforces our commitment to developing talent across disciplines, from shipping and logistics to specialised education and community services.

For companies, our training programme doubles as a vetting mechanism. Every graduate has been assessed across technical knowledge, communication, work ethic, and professional readiness. When a company hires from our cohort, they are not hiring a stranger with a degree. They are hiring someone who has already proven themselves in an intensive, real-world environment.

What We Do

Three Pillars of Impact

01 / Coaching

Train. Assess. Place.

Our flagship bootcamps take fresh graduates and career changers through intensive, hands-on training delivered by industry veterans. Participants are assessed on technical knowledge, communication, and professional readiness. Top graduates are placed directly with hiring companies from our network of over 1,000 corporate connections. Coaching is the entry point to the Cargonomics ecosystem: the vetting mechanism that companies trust.

02 / Connecting

Events. Networks. Opportunities.

Cargonomics brings the shipping and logistics industry together through conferences, networking events, and business matching. Our inaugural conference at The Reverie Saigon drew over 300 attendees. These events create the relationships that power placements, partnerships, and industry growth. Connecting is the ecosystem that surrounds our training programmes.

03 / Consulting

Strategy. Insight. Growth.

For shipping and logistics businesses looking to grow, we offer strategic advisory services: market studies, feasibility analysis, financial evaluation, and operational consulting. Our consulting work is grounded in decades of hands-on industry leadership, not theory. Consulting is the revenue expansion arm that completes the Cargonomics model.
