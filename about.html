About Cargonomics

Coaching. Consulting. Connecting.

Cargonomics is where the shipping and logistics world comes to grow. We are a specialized partner for the industry, with a dedicated platform for Coaching, Consulting, and Connecting.

Driven by a team of dedicated professionals with over 50 years of combined experience, extensive international partnerships, and a strong local network, we help individuals and organizations navigate the complexities of global trade. Our solutions include expert-led training and courses, strategic consulting, recruitment and placement, business matching, and exclusive industry events.

We Coach, assess and match aspiring graduates to meaningful and compatible jobs, as well as train and elevate competencies of working professionals.

We Connect shipping and logistics professionals locally and internationally through meaningful networking events.

We Consult companies based on our specialised knowledge, local know-how, and independent views.

Logistics is a global engine. Cargonomics provides the human element that keeps it running.

Our mission: To build a progressive and empowering knowledge-based company, empowering the Vietnamese shipping and logistics community through expert coaching, strategic consulting, and elite networking, setting a new global standard for local excellence.

Visit us: 268 To Hien Thanh Street, Hoa Hung Ward, District 10, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

What We Do

We Coach. We Connect. We Consult.

01 / Coaching

Train. Assess. Place.

Our flagship bootcamps take fresh graduates and career changers through intensive, hands-on training delivered by industry veterans. Participants are assessed on technical knowledge, communication, and professional readiness. Top graduates are placed directly with leading logistics companies like Gemadept, Vina Logistics, and Saigon Port, drawn from our network of over 1,000 corporate connections.

02 / Connecting

Events. Networks. Opportunities.

Cargonomics brings the shipping and logistics industry together through conferences, networking events, and business matching. Our inaugural conference at The Reverie Saigon drew over 300 attendees. These events create the relationships that power placements, partnerships, and industry growth.

03 / Consulting

Strategy. Insight. Growth.

For shipping and logistics businesses looking to grow, we offer strategic advisory services: market studies, feasibility analysis, financial evaluation, and operational consulting. Our consulting work is grounded in decades of hands-on industry leadership, not theory.

Our Team

Leadership

Abraham Y. Elias

Co-Founder / Coach

With over 25 years of leadership in global shipping and logistics, Elias brings unmatched industry depth to Cargonomics. His career spans nearly two decades at APL (now part of CMA CGM Group), where he held regional leadership roles across Southeast Asia, Oceania, Sri Lanka, and India. He co-founded Cargonomics to apply his network and domain expertise to developing the next generation of shipping professionals.

Anthony Tay

Co-Founder / Coach

With over 25 years in international shipping and logistics, Anthony brings deep operational and commercial expertise to Cargonomics. His career spans nearly two decades at APL Logistics, including 7 years as Country Director of APL Logistics Vietnam and senior global roles in Singapore as Global Head of International Order Management, VP International Order Management and Forwarding Services, and Director of Sales and Marketing Asia. Earlier he led sales and marketing for Panalpina across Asia. He now runs EvlvEdge Advisory, a Singapore-based consultancy specialising in international freight, origin services, and digital supply chain transformation. Anthony holds a Bachelor of Supply Chain Management from Curtin University.
