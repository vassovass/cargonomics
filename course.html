Curriculum

What You'll Learn

Module 01

Foundations

Understand why global trade exists, how Vietnam operates as an export-driven economy, and how the supply chain connects factories, ports, carriers, and customers across borders.

Module 02

Ecosystem

Learn who does what in the logistics ecosystem: ports and terminal operators, shipping lines and airlines, freight forwarders and 3PLs, inland transport, customs, and regulatory authorities.

Module 03

Incoterms: The Big 3

Master the language of shipping: Incoterms (EXW, FOB, CIF), cost responsibility, risk transfer, and the operational vocabulary (BL, AWB, FCL/LCL, CY/CFS) used in every logistics role.

Module 04

Movers

Explore courier, air, ocean, and inland transport. Understand when and why each mode is used, then follow a real shipment from factory floor to final customer.

Module 05

Cargo & Containers

Identify container types (dry, reefer, flat rack, open top, tank), cargo categories (general, refrigerated, dangerous goods, oversized), and the equipment that moves them.

Module 06

Negotiable & Non-Negotiable

Navigate the paperwork that keeps trade moving: commercial invoices, bills of lading, customs declarations, certificates of origin. Learn why documentation accuracy is essential to clearance, delivery, and payment.

Module 07

Money Matters

Follow the money through global trade: how payments, letters of credit, freight pricing, and risk flow between buyer, seller, bank, and carrier. Finance is where shipping either works or breaks.

Module 08

Boot Camp & Beyond

From selection to onboarding and beyond: the port excursion, your certification, placement support, and the Cargonomics alumni network that stays with you through your career.

Eligibility

Who Should Apply

We are not looking for just anyone. We are looking for the next generation of logistics leaders. We hand-select 30 students based on academic excellence and a clear ambition to build a long-term career in shipping.

  • Academic Excellence: Final year students, fresh graduates, first-time job-seekers, and candidates interested in the shipping and logistics industry
  • Available for the full 5-day program (no partial attendance)
  • Based in or willing to travel to Ho Chi Minh City
  • Comfortable learning in English (training is conducted in English)
We review every application individually. Limited to 30-40 students. This is an invitation to apply, not a guarantee of a place.

Program Structure

Bootcamp Schedule

Day 1
Trade Foundations & Industry Ecosystem

Global trade fundamentals, Vietnam's role as an export-driven economy, and the full logistics ecosystem: ports, carriers, forwarders, 3PLs, customs, and where the jobs are.

Day 2
Incoterms, Transport & Equipment

Incoterms and risk transfer, transport modes and when to use each, container types and cargo categories. Follow a real shipment from factory to final customer.

Day 3
Documentation, Careers & Ethics

Shipping documentation and information flow. Entry-level roles, career paths, skills employers value, professional ethics, and how AI is reshaping the industry. Interview preparation and placement readiness.

Day 4
Port Excursion & Industry Immersion

A full day at a working port facility. See operations first-hand, meet professionals on the ground, and understand what a career in logistics looks like in practice.
